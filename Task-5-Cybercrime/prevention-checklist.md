# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Cyber Safety Checklist

As college students, we spend a large part of our lives online — for studies, payments, and communication. Following basic cybersecurity practices can help prevent fraud, identity theft, and data loss.

## ✅ Do’s (Safe Practices)

* 🔐 Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
* 📱 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
* 🌐 Always verify website URLs before entering login details.
* 📧 Be cautious of suspicious emails or messages asking for personal information.
* 💻 Keep your devices and apps updated regularly.
* 🔒 Log out from shared or public computers after use.
* 💸 Always double-check the receiver’s details before making UPI payments.
* 📲 Use only official apps for banking and payments.

---

## ❌ Don’ts (Avoid These Mistakes)

* ❌ Do not share your OTP, passwords, or PIN with anyone.
* ❌ Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links.
* ❌ Do not download files from untrusted websites.
* ❌ Never use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
* ❌ Do not save passwords on shared devices.
* ❌ Avoid responding to messages that create urgency or panic.
* ❌ Do not accept unknown payment requests on UPI apps.
* ❌ Never scan random QR codes for receiving money (this can lead to fraud).

---

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime in India

If you become a victim of cybercrime:

* 🌐 Report online at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
* 📞 Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930 (24x7)**

---

## 💡 Final Note

Staying alert and informed is the best defense against cyber threats. A few careful steps can save you from major financial and personal loss.
