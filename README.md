## Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic (Module 1)
Created a one-page infographic using **Canva** covering three key topics: what digital literacy is, safe internet practices, and professional online presence. The design uses visual icons and structured sections to help peers quickly understand core digital literacy concepts.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio (Module 2)
Set up professional profiles on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Kaggle**. Each platform serves a different purpose — GitHub for code and projects, LinkedIn for professional networking, and Kaggle for data science practice. These profiles form the foundation of my digital identity as a student and future professional.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms (Module 3)
Completed a beginner challenge on **HackerRank** (Python Introduction) and built a **Google Form** with 5 questions to serve as a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz for batchmates. The quiz covers topics from all five modules of this course.

> 📋 **Digital Literacy Quiz Form Link:** (https://forms.gle/FcX6TVRafGnfz3Qi8)

### Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette (Module 4)
Drafted two professional emails — one requesting an assignment deadline extension and one expressing interest in a summer internship. Also created a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist covering responsible online behaviour for college students.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness (Module 5)
Researched and wrote a case study on **UPI/Online Payment Fraud**, including how it happens step-by-step, who is targeted, and the consequences. Produced a Stay Safe Online prevention checklist with 8+ actionable tips tailored for college students in India, including UPI safety and reporting via cybercrime.gov.in / Helpline 1930.
